Elder Ministry

Our Elder Members Ministry supports senior adults through fellowship, prayer, and spiritual care





Day & Time





Thursday

⏰ 10:00 AM – 11:30 AM

📍 Church Hall / Fellowship Hall





What We Do





• Conducts regular prayer meetings and Bible reading sessions

• Organizes fellowship gatherings to strengthen church community

• Carries out home visits and hospital visits for care and encouragement

• Provides counseling and spiritual support to church members





Elder Ministry Leader

Pastor David Thompson

Pastor David leads our Elder Members Ministry with love and compassion, ensuring our senior members receive the care and support they need.
