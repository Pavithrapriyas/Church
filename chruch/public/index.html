About Us




Our church was built on faith, prayer, and a deep desire to reflect Christ’s love in our community. What began as a small gathering of believers has grown into a vibrant place of worship, fellowship, and spiritual growth. We welcome individuals and families of all ages to experience God’s presence, strengthen their faith, and walk together in hope and unity.
